The Kultur & Kongresshaus Aarau, located at Schlossplatz in Aarau, is a distinguished venue that we have the pleasure of hosting our event at. 

This venue combines architectural elegance with modern functionality, making it an excellent choice for a variety of gatherings. The facility boasts a generous area of 1000 square meters, adaptable for different configurations and event sizes, ranging from intimate seminars to larger assemblies. 

The interior of Kultur & Kongresshaus Aarau is characterized by its high ceilings on the ground floor, which provide a sense of openness and grandeur. The venue is equipped with advanced event technology, ensuring that all technical needs—from sound to lighting—are seamlessly met. 

A spacious foyer will serve as a welcoming area and exhibition space, adding to the venue's versatility. For convenience, the venue is located near the heart of Aarau’s historic center, surrounded by dining and leisure options, which makes it not only a prime spot for local attendees but also appealing for visitors from all over switzerland
